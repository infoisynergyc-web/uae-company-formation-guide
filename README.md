## Types of Company Formation in the UAE

### Mainland Company Formation
UAE mainland companies allow businesses to operate anywhere in the UAE and work directly with the local market. This option is ideal for businesses that require flexibility and government contracts.

### Freezone Company Formation
Freezone company formation offers 100% foreign ownership, tax benefits, and simplified setup procedures. It is suitable for startups, trading, and service-based businesses.

### Offshore Company Formation
Offshore companies are best for asset protection, international trading, and holding structures. They do not require physical office space in the UAE.
